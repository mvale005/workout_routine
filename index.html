7-Day Workout Routine

Day 1: Cardio + Upper Body

Warm-Up: 5-10 min walk + dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings)

Cardio: 20 min run/walk (2 min walk, 1 min run, repeat)

Strength:

  • Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 40 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps/side
  • Pull-Up Negatives: 2 sets of 5-8 reps

Stretch: Chest, triceps, hamstring (5-10 min)

Day 2: Lower Body + Light Cardio

Warm-Up: 5-10 min walk + dynamic stretches

Cardio: 15 min brisk walk or light jog

Strength:

  • Goblet Squats (kettlebell): 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps/leg
  • Kettlebell Deadlifts: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Nordic Hamstrings: 3 sets of 5 reps

Stretch: Quad, calf, hip flexor (5-10 min)

Day 3: Running Focus

Warm-Up: 5-10 min walk + dynamic stretches

Cardio: 25 min run/walk (3 min walk, 2 min run, repeat)

Strength: None

Stretch: Full-body (hamstrings, calves, quads, back) (10 min)

Day 4: Full Body + Light Cardio

Warm-Up: 5-10 min walk + dynamic stretches

Cardio: 15 min brisk walk or light jog

Strength:

  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Kettlebell Swings: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Pull-Up Bar Hanging Knee Raises: 2 sets of 10-12 reps

Stretch: Shoulder, lower back, hip (5-10 min)

Day 5: Active Recovery

Warm-Up: 5 min light walk

Cardio: 20-30 min easy walk or slow jog

Strength: Optional: 10 squats, 10 push-ups

Stretch: Full-body (focus on tight areas: hamstrings, shoulders, hips) (10-15 min)

Day 6: Cardio + Upper Body

Warm-Up: 5-10 min walk + dynamic stretches

Cardio: 20-25 min run/walk (2 min run, 1 min walk, repeat)

Strength:

  • Dumbbell Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 40 reps
  • Tricep Overhead Extensions: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Pull-Up Bar Hold: 2 sets of 15-30 sec

Stretch: Arms, chest, calf (5-10 min)

Day 7: Lower Body

Warm-Up: 5-10 min walk + dynamic stretches

Cardio: None

Strength:

  • Kettlebell Step-Ups (bench): 3 sets of 10 reps/leg
  • Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Nordic Hamstrings: 3 sets of 5 reps

Stretch: Lower body focus (hamstrings, quads, glutes) (10-15 min)
